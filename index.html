Privilege Deodorant Spray for Men

I decided to pull five players from a painting of basketball players for the main theme and I used images of basketball players playing basketball and people cheering for the background. I added an audio clip of people cheering and another audio clip of an upbeat soundtrack to complete the project.

Applications: Adobe After Effects, Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator

Pepperidge Farm “Just My Imagination”

Using images that I found on the internet I decided on an image of a rooster and another image of a dairy cow. I wanted to show the cow enjoying a snack of milk and cookies. The idea was to have the milk and cookies disappear with the cow acting non-cholent as if he has no idea what happen to the milk and cookies… I added a milk mustache on the cow, a barnyard sound clip and a country music audio clip to complete the project.

Applications: Adobe After Effects, Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator

Folgers Coffeehouse Blend “Coffee Time”

For this project I decided to create several animated coffee cups dancing and spinning around with a video in the background of people drinking and enjoying Folgers Coffee. At the end of the video I wanted to show one of the animated coffee cup also drinking a cup of coffee.

Applications: Adobe After Effects, Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator
